# Computational Social Science Methods

Methodological reflections and toolbuilding for digital trace data, social network analysis, Facebook Ads-based survey recruitment, and the ethics of using online social data. Includes CooRnet and downstream open-source contributions, and underpins the PI's PhD-level teaching.
