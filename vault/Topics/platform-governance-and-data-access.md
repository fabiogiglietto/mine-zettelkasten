# Platform Governance and Data Access

Critical analysis of platform policies, algorithmic governance, content moderation, and the shifting landscape of social media research APIs in the DSA era. Covers Meta's political content reduction, the CrowdTangle shutdown, and the methodological consequences for independent research.
