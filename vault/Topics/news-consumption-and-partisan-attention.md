# News Consumption and Partisan Attention

Analyses of how news circulates and is engaged with on Facebook and Instagram, including partisan alignment, journalistic quality, insularity, cross-partisan exposure, and the European newsfeed. Includes work on the Multi-Party Media Partisanship Attention Score.
