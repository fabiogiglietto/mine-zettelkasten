# Social Media Research Methods and Digital Traces

Methodological work on using digital traces — Facebook ads for survey recruitment, URL Shares datasets, user-sequence mining, multi-platform analysis — as research instruments, including their limits, ethics, and reproducibility. Bridges the PI's long-standing engagement with computational social science.
